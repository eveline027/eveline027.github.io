About Me

My name is Eveline from Indonesia. I am an undergraduate in Computer Engineering from Binus University, Jakarta. My journey as a teacher started when I become a mentor for my junior back in university. Little did I know that after graduation I will become a teacher. My first job was as a tuition teacher teaching Computer Science for the Cambridge IGCSE examination. I worked at a tuition center for 6 months after that I convert to private teaching going home to home.
In 2021 in the middle of the pandemic, I started working as a Coding Teacher for kids ranging from 6 to 18 years old. I have taught around 150 students varying from private classes, group classes, also after-school activities. The topic that I teach is Scratch Programming, HTML5 and CSS3, and Basic Python.
